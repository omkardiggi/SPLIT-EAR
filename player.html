      NOT HEARING THE SPLIT?

      • Connect earbuds first, in your device settings. Websites can't pair Bluetooth audio — pair in your phone/PC Bluetooth menu, and this app plays through whatever output is active.
      • Wear stereo headphones or earbuds. Phone and laptop speakers blend both channels in the air, so the split won't be audible.
      • Turn off "Mono Audio". On iOS: Settings → Accessibility → Audio/Visual. On Android: Settings → Accessibility → Audio adjustment. Mono mode merges both ears into one signal.
      • Check Bluetooth mode. Some earbuds fall back to a mono "call" profile when the mic is active. Close voice apps and reconnect.
      • Using tab capture? In the share dialog, choose the Chrome Tab option (not Window or Screen) and tick "Also share tab audio" — otherwise no sound reaches the app. Desktop Chrome/Edge only.
      • Balance slider centered. An off-center L/R balance in system settings will mute one side.
